#### Plan Differences
Total plan nodes: Comet 151 → Gluten 191 (+40); edges 167 → 189 (+22).

Operator family swaps (Comet → Gluten Velox):
- Scans: 11 `CometNativeScan` → 11 `FileSourceScanExecTransformer` (catalog_sales×1, customer×2, date_dim×3, item×1, store_sales×3, web_sales×1).
- Joins: 15 `CometSortMergeJoin` and 4 `CometBroadcastHashJoin` → 15 `ShuffledHashJoinExecTransformer` and 4 `BroadcastHashJoinExecTransformer`. Comet picks SMJ for the large fact-fact / fact-dim joins; Gluten picks ShuffledHashJoin (no pre-shuffle sort). Both have the same broadcast-hash-join count.
- Sorts: Comet has 26 `CometSort` operators; Gluten has 0 (no `SortExecTransformer`). All sort operators disappear because SMJ → SHJ.
- Aggregates: 20 `CometHashAggregate` → 13 `RegularHashAggregateExecTransformer` + 3 `FlushableHashAggregateExecTransformer` (16 total) plus 36 `ProjectExecTransformer` and 17 `FilterExecTransformer` (vs 25 `CometProject` / 17 `CometFilter`).
- Shuffles/exchanges: 13 `CometExchange` → 13 `ColumnarExchange`; 3 `CometBroadcastExchange` → 3 `ColumnarBroadcastExchange`; 3 `CometSubqueryBroadcast` → 4 `ColumnarSubqueryBroadcast`; 1 `CometUnion` → 1 `ColumnarUnion`; 1 `CometTakeOrderedAndProject` → 1 `TakeOrderedAndProjectExecTransformer`. Gluten additionally adds 13 `VeloxResizeBatches`, 27 `InputIteratorTransformer`, 19 `WholeStageCodegenTransformer`, and 2 `VeloxColumnarToRow` boundaries that are not present in Comet.
- AdaptiveSparkPlan nodes: 5 → 6 (+1); ReusedExchange 2 → 3 (+1).

#### Root Cause
Comet's optimiser converts q23b's three large fact-fact / fact-dim joins into `CometSortMergeJoin`, which forces 26 `CometSort` operators on the shuffled inputs. The sort cost concentrates in the post-shuffle reducer stages: the 200-task stages 810, 813 and 798 each spend 17–28 seconds per task processing only 260–600 MB of shuffle input, accumulating 13.97M ms (≈3.4× of Gluten's three corresponding reducer stages combined, 5.06M ms).

Gluten replaces all 15 SMJs with `ShuffledHashJoinExecTransformer` (and keeps the 4 broadcast-hash joins), eliminating every sort. The same logical join graph is executed as hash joins inside Velox columnar pipelines, with `WholeStageCodegenTransformer` (×19), `InputIteratorTransformer` (×27), and `VeloxResizeBatches` (×13) producing more, finer-grained stages (48 vs 19, 32137 vs 10545 tasks) and 5× more shuffle bytes (322 GB vs 64 GB). Despite that extra shuffle work, per-task throughput is ~5× higher on the dominant reducer stages — Gluten's 11114/11117 process 118 GB at 8.0–8.6s/task while Comet's 798 processes 51 GB at 17.7s/task. Total stage time falls from 6m49.561s to 3m54.443s, putting Gluten Velox 2.08× ahead at the wall clock (227.175s → 109.464s, -53%). Spill is 0 B and GC ≤649 ms in both runs, so the gap is entirely in compute: SMJ+sort in Comet's columnar engine versus SHJ in Velox's vectorised pipelines.
